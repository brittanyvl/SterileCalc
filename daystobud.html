Days until Best Use Date

All sterile medication will have a Best Use Date. If you are purchasing from a commercial distributor you likely will have a contract stipulating 90 to 180 days of shelf life, however, if you source compounded sterile drugs the Best Use Date may be much shorter. You can use this calculator to determine how many days exist between today's date and the pharmacy or manufacturer's best use date.
